## 📖 **Description**

**PsycheMirror** is an **EEG-based mind-to-image system** that translates **neural activity** into **visual representations of thought**.

By capturing electrical patterns from the brain's **visual** and **associative cortices**, PsycheMirror reconstructs mental imagery — from *fleeting memories* and *imagined scenes* to *abstract concepts* — and renders them as **viewable digital images**.

Acting as a **true mirror of the psyche**, the system decodes how the mind internally *"sees,"* offering a **non-invasive window into subjective experience**.
